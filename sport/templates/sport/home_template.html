Welcome to Sporting!

What is Sporting?

Sporting is a Website that allow you to Subscribe to any Sport in the Club Online.

How do I Subscribe to a Sport?

First, you need to Register in to create a New Account or Login if you already have one. Then, click Add Sport and choose the Sport and Session you want from the Form that will appear.

What are some other Facilities at Sporting?

  • You can view the Details about each Sport you are Subscribed to.
  • You can view your Schedule all along the Week.
  • You can view your Medical Report.
  • You can Look up your Financial Status.
